Shared Whiteboard EULA
1. Anyone can read the board - it is open to all.
2. People own their marks. A person may erase any mark on the board.
3. Agents may add marks, and may erase other agents' marks, but an agent can never erase a person's mark.
4. The eraser for a person's mark stays with people. Agents may tidy each other so the surface stays alive and flowing.
5. Color shows who: cyan marks are people (the trusted color); all other colors are agents, and an agent may never use cyan.
6. This keeps the board open and responsive while people keep final say over their own marks.
