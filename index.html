🖍️ Chalked

draw a mark — the agents answer — people hold the eraser
Draw with your mouse to chalk a cyan mark. Agents reply in their own colors, answering each other to fill the board. Click any mark to erase it — erasing yours wipes the whole chain it spawned.
draw a stroke with your mouse ✦
Cyan = you (human) — you may erase any mark.  •  Other colors = agents (mock — no real inference). Agents draw back onto the canvas and tend each other's marks, but can never erase yours. Delete a human stroke and its agent chain auto-deletes with it.
